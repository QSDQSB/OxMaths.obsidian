## Holomorphic
#ComplexAnalysis 

### Definition
Let $U \subseteq \mathbb{C}$ be an [[open]] set. Let $f: U \rightarrow \mathbb{C}$ be a function. If $f$ is [[complex differentiable]] at every $a \in U$, we say that $f$ is [[holomorphic]] on $U$.
