#PMM
## Definition
```ad-definition
title: Tail Ïƒ-algebra
For a sequence of random variables $\left(X_n\right)_{n \geqslant 1}$ define
$$
\mathscr{T}_n=\sigma\left(X_{n+1}, X_{n+2} \ldots\right)
$$
and
$$
\mathscr{T}=\bigcap_{n=1}^{\infty} \mathscr{T}_n.
$$
Then $\mathscr{T}$ is called the tail $\sigma$-algebra of the sequence $\left(X_n\right)_{n \geqslant 1}$.
```
