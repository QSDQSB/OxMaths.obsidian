```ad-definition
title: Bias
The [[bias]] of an [[Estimate|estimator]] $T$ for $\gamma=g(\theta)$ is
$$
\operatorname{bias}(T, \theta)=\mathbb{E}_\theta[T]-g(\theta) .
$$
$T$ is called [[Bias|unbiased]] for $g(\theta)$ if $\mathbb{E}_\theta[T]=g(\theta) \forall \theta \in \Theta$.
```
